GENERAL INSTRUCTIONS

1. Reach your allotted centre on {{ et.dated.strftime('%d.%m.%Y') if et and et.dated else '28.06.2025' }} by 8:00 am.
2. Mark your attendance and collect duty cards from staff at the gate. Your duty room will be mentioned on duty cards/attendance sheets.
3. Please report to your Centre Supdt. immediately for instructions in one of the rooms/ hall (between 8:30-8:45 am).
4. Display seating arrangement on the entrance door of your room. Check roll number slips on the seats. It should match with seating plan. It may either be line pattern or as zig zag pattern. If any problem, pl. contact your Dy. Supdt.
5. The examination work will start at 10:00 am. The candidates will fill up the OMR sheets Side-I and complete other formalities (attendance, thumb impression, Q.B. no., Q.B series etc.) between 10:00 am to 10:30 am.
6. The program wise timings of test will be :
Ph.D. 10:30 am to 12:30 pm 30 minutes are for completing pre exam activities
and 2 hours exam

Kindly follow the timing schedule strictly as per respective programme in your centre.

7. Allow the entry of the candidates at 9:00 am.
8. Only those candidates possessing VALID ADMIT CARDS of your room and whose roll numbers figure in the list of a particular room will be allowed. Do not allow the candidates to enter in the room without colour photo duly self attested on the admit card.
9. Kindly check while candidates entering in your room that he/she does not possess any mobile or objectionable items (as listed at Sr. No. 11) in the room.
10. No candidate will be allowed to enter the centre 30 minutes after the start of examination.
11. Following items are not allowed to carry inside the examination centre/examination room under any circumstances; all ornaments/jewellery, religious symbol etc., any metallic item personal belongings like bag etc., wrist watch, camera, calculator, mobile phone, pager, blue tooth, earphones, purse, log tables, health band, electronic gadgets with or without built-in-calculators, geometry/plastic pouch, blank or printed papers, written chits etc.
Candidates found possessing any of the these items will CONSTITUTE AS USE OF UNFAIR MEANS AND WILL BE DISQUALIFIED FOR HIS/HER RIGHT TO ADMISSION.
12. No arrangement will be made at the centre for keeping any articles/ items belonging to the candidates.
13. Please ensure that candidates should not have goggles/sunglasses and caps during examination. Please ensure that candidate should not have face covered with cloth/mask during photography.
14. In case of any discrepancy/enquiry regarding the roll number/Centre/Admit Card etc. contact the Centre Superintendent.
15. Invigilator will sign on the Admit Card of the candidate after duly verification of his/her identity.
16. The candidate will hand over OMR Sheet & Admit Card (University copy) to the Invigilator(s) before leaving the examination hall.

18. Photography of the candidates will be taken with their photography roll number sheet.
19. No candidate should be allowed to leave the room before the end of the examination. A candidate may, however, be allowed to leave the room under very exceptional circumstances such as serious illness with the prior permission of the Centre Supdt.
20. Please ensure that candidates 'absent' be marked with RED pen on the attendance sheet by encircling the same.

BEFORE AND DURING THE TEST

1. Ensure that the candidates are seated according to seating plan on his/her allotted seat.
2. Distribute the OMR sheets at 10:00 am as per seating plan and instruct the candidates for its proper filling.
3. Take the attendance, thumb impression (left for male and right for female), QB no., QB series etc. on prescribed proforma between 10:00 am to 10:30 am.
4. Sealed packets of the question booklets will be opened by the Invigilators 5 minutes (10:25 am) before the start of the examination. Please ensure that the order in which the OMR/Question booklets are arranged is not disturbed.
5. Distribute Question booklets and OMR sheets strictly as per the prescribed procedure (explained during the instructions) and as per list. It will be in ascending order.
6. If any candidate is absent, leave question booklet and OMR sheet on his/her seat. Collect those 30 minutes after the start of the test.
7. Direct the candidates to fill up his/her particulars on the OMR sheet with black/blue pens only. No pencil or other colour is allowed.
8. Direct the candidates to check the question booklet thoroughly before writing their roll numbers on them. If it is defective, the same should be replaced by the one having the same series e.g. A be replaced by A only.
9. Make announcement in your room that If, by chance, a question is unanswerable or having multiple answers, such questions shall be judged by experts for any errors (post exam moderation) and due advantage would be given to all the candidates.
10. Direct the candidates to fill up the roll numbers in words and figures on the question booklets also.
11. Both the invigilator(s) must check thoroughly the roll numbers of the candidate both on question booklets and OMR sheets, and thereafter should put their signatures. Any OMR found signed by invigilator (s) without checking or not having roll numbers or without dark circles or proper roll numbers are liable for strict action by the authority.
12. The candidates should be informed that questions are to be attempted on OMR sheets by filling/darkening the circle as described in the instructions on admit card.
13. Collect UNUSED question booklets and OMR sheets of absentees 30 minutes after the start of examination and hand over these to the Dy. Supdt.
14. No candidate should be allowed to leave the room before the end of the examination. A candidate may, however, be allowed to leave the room under very exceptional circumstances such as serious illness with the prior permission of the Centre Supdt.
15. The members of the photography team carrying appropriate Duty Cards are allowed entry in the room for photography.

AFTER THE TEST

1. The Invigilators will collect the USED OMR sheets and ADMIT CARDS at the end of examination.
2. Please arrange OMR sheets roll number-wise and hand over these to the Dy. Supdt. The invigilator(s) must ensure that the number of Used/ Unused Question Booklets, Used/ Unused OMR sheets must tally with the number of candidates listed as present in the attendance sheet.
3. Hand over the following materials to Dy. Supdt.
i. Attendance sheets
ii. Envelopes of Used and Unused OMR sheets
iii. Envelopes of Used/Unused Question booklets
iv. Envelope of photography roll numbers
v. Envelope of Admit Cards
vi. Check list duly signed
vii. All other materials related to exam.
